The result of a Contains evaluation — the question judged, the verdict, the Kookie pin, the schema version, and a timestamp. The question is a putative fact ([[putative-fact]]): a subject set paired with one subject, held as a claim to judge; a set-valued question is unrepresentable. Complete at birth and replayable; not a bare boolean.
