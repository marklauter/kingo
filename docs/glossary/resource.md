The namespaced object a fact is about — namespace:resource-id, such as doc:readme. It anchors facts and has no stored state of its own. Also a legal fact member — the resource itself, written `folder:y#...`, the object-object edge the fact-to-subject-set walk traverses ([[fact]]).
